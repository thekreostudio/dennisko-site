Dennis Ko

Legacy Advocate · Speaker on Entrepreneurship & Family Business Values · Preserving the Legacy of Gonzalo Co It

Professional Profile

Dennis Ko is a legacy advocate and speaker dedicated to preserving and articulating the entrepreneurial philosophy of his grandfather, Gonzalo Co It — visionary founder of Gonzalo Laboratory (now Green Cross, Inc.), the company behind the iconic Philippine household brands Green Cross Alcohol and Zonrox Bleach.

Through keynote talks, business forums, podcast features, and leadership engagements, he shares timeless lessons drawn from firsthand family history and documented accounts in The Green Cross Saga. His advocacy centers on resilience, humility, financial discipline, ethical entrepreneurship, and the importance of safeguarding one's business legacy.

Dennis speaks to entrepreneurs, professionals, and family business owners on topics spanning entrepreneurship, governance, continuity planning, and purpose-driven leadership — drawing inspiration from a founder who began in 1952 with only ₱3,400 in savings and built one of the Philippines' most trusted hygiene brands.
